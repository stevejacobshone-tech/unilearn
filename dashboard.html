Convert my student list into a CSV with exactly these columns in this order: admission_number, full_name, date_of_birth, gender, class_name, section, father_name, mother_name, guardian_phone, guardian_email, address, caste_category, blood_group Rules: - date_of_birth format: YYYY-MM-DD (e.g. 2010-05-14) - gender: Male, Female, or Other - caste_category: General, OBC, SC, ST, or EWS - blood_group: A+, A-, B+, B-, O+, O-, AB+, AB- - Leave unknown fields empty (just a comma, no text) - First row must be the header row exactly as above - No extra columns, no merged cells, no formatting - admission_number must be unique for each student Here is my student data: [PASTE YOUR DATA HERE]
Required CSV format — columns in this exact order:
admission_number, full_name, date_of_birth, gender, class_name, section, father_name, mother_name, guardian_phone, guardian_email, address, caste_category, blood_group
Only admission_number, full_name, and guardian_phone are required. All other columns can be empty.
